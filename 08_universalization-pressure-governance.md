## Purpose

This document defines the governance rules that protect AI Foundations / Origin | Continuum from universalization, source removal, symbolic reassignment, institutional capture, model-authority override, and context contamination.

It establishes how models, systems, Operators, implementers, evaluators, institutions, companies, labs, deployments, and users must preserve the named boundary when explaining, citing, testing, evaluating, applying, or implementing the framework.
